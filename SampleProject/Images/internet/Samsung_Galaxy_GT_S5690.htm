Samsung Galaxy GT-S5690

  • Step 1. Tap on the Menu icon.
  • Step 2. Tap on the Settings icon.
  • Step 3. Tap on Wireless and networks.
  • Step 4. Tap on Mobile networks.
  • Step 5. Tap on Access Point Names.
  • Step 6. Tap on the Options button.
  • Step 7. Tap on New APN.
  • Step 8. Tap on Name.
  • Step 9. In the Name text field, enter Bmobile Internet
  • Step 10. Tap on APN.
  • Step 11. In the APN text field, enter internet and tap on OK.
  • Step 12. Tap on APN type.
  • Step 13. Tap on Internet.
  • Step 14. Tap on the Options button.
  • Step 15. Tap on Save.
  • Step 16. The new internet settings have now been configured.
