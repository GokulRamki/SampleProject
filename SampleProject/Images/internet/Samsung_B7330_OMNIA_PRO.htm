Samsung B7330 OMNIA PRO

 

  • Step 1. Press the Start button.
  • Step 2. Highlight the Settings icon and press the Center button.
  • Step 3. Highlight Connections and press the Center button.
  • Step 4. Highlight GPRS and press the Center button.
  • Step 5. Highlight NEW and press the Center button.
  • Step 6. Under the Description field enter in Bmobile Internet
  • Step 7. Highlight the Connects to field press the Center button.
  • Step 8. Highlight The Internet and press the Center button.
  • Step 9. In the Access point field enter internet and save.
  • Step 10. Your manual internet has been configured.
