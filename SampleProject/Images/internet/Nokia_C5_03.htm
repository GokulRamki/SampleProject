Nokia C5-03

  • Step 1. Press the Menu button.
  • Step 2. Highlight the Settings icon and press the Open button.
  • Step 3. Highlight Settings and press the Open button.
  • Step 4. Highlight Connection and press the Open button.
  • Step 5. Highlight Destinations and press the Open button.
  • Step 6. Highlight Internet and press the Open button.
  • Step 7. Press the Add button.
  • Step 8. Press the No button.
  • Step 9. Highlight Packet data and press the Select button.
  • Step 10. Under the Packet data access point name field enter Bmobile Internet and press the OK button.
  • Step 11. Press the Edit button.
  • Step 12. Highlight Access point name and press the Change button.
  • Step 13. Change the Access point name to internet and press the OK button.
  • Step 14. Highlight Homepage and press the Change button.
  • Step 15. Change the Homepage to http://www.bmobile.com.pg and press the OK button.
  • Step 16. Your profile has been created.
