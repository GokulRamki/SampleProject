Nokia E5

  • Step 1. Press the Menu button.
  • Step 2. Highlight the Ctrl.panel icon and press the Center button.
  • Step 3. Highlight Settings and press the Center button.
  • Step 4. Highlight Connection and press the Center button.
  • Step 5. Highlight Destinations and press the Center button.
  • Step 6. Highlight Internet and press the Center button.
  • Step 7. Press the Center button.
  • Step 8. Press the Yes button.
  • Step 9. Highlight Packet data and press the Select button.
  • Step 10. In the Packet data access point name field enter Bmobile Internet and press the OK button.
  • Step 11. Press the Center button.
  • Step 12. Highlight Access point name and press the Center button.
  • Step 13. In the Access point name field internet and press the OK button.
  • Step 14. Highlight Homepage and press the Center button.
  • Step 15. In the Homepage field enter http://www.bmobile.com.pg and press the OK button.
  • Step 16. Press the Back button until you reach the Access point screen.
  • Step 17. Your Internet profile will now be configured.
