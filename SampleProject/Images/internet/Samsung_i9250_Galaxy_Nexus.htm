Samsung i9250 Galaxy Nexus

  • Step 1. Tap on the Menu icon, then on the Settings icon.
  • Step 2. Tap on More and then Tap on the Mobile networks option.
  • Step 3. Tap on Access Point Names
  • Step 4. Tap on the Options icon located at the lower right corner of the screen.
  • Step 5. Tap on the New APN option to create new APn.
  • Step 6. Tap on the Name field.
  • Step 7. Enter Bmobile Internet in the Name field and tap on the OK option.
  • Step 8. Tap on the APN field.
  • Step 9. Enter internet in the APN field and tap on the OK option.
  • Step 10. Tap on the APN type field.
  • Step 11. Enter default in the APN type field and tap on the OK option.
  • Step 16. Tap on the Options icon on the handset.
  • Step 17. Tap on the Save option to save the profile.
  • Step 18. Tap on the Bmobile Internet selection circle to see a green light appear in the circle.
  • Step 19. Your profile will be created and set as default.
