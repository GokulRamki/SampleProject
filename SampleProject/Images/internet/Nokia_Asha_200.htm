Nokia Asha 200

  • Step1. Press the Menu button.
  • Step2. Highlight Settings and press the Select button.
  • Step3. Highlight Configuration and press the Select button.
  • Step4. Highlight Personal settings and press the Select button.
  • Step5. Press the Add button.
  • Step6. Highlight Web and press the Select button.
  • Step7. Highlight Account name and press the Select button.
  • Step8. Under the Account name field enter Bmobile Internet and press the OK button.
  • Step9. Highlight Use preferred access pt and press the select button.
  • Step10. Highlight No and press the Select button.
  • Step11. Highlight Access point settings and press the Select button.
  • Step12. Highlight Bearer settings and press the Select button.
  • Step13. Highlight Packet data access pt and press the Select button.
  • Step14. In the Access point field, enter internet and press the OK button.
  • Step15. Press the Back button until you reach the Personal accounts screen.
  • Step16. Highlight Bmobile Internet and press the Options button.
  • Step17. Highlight Activate and press the Select button.
  • Step18. Your manual internet will be configured.
