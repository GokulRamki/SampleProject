Samsung - N7000 Galaxy Note

  • Step 1. Tap on the Applications icon.
  • Step 2. Tap on the Settings icon.
  • Step 3. Tap on Wireless and network.
  • Step 4. Tap on Mobile networks.
  • Step 5. Tap on Access Point Names.
  • Step 6. Tap on the Options icon on the handset.
  • Step 7. Tap on New APn.
  • Step 8. Tap on the Name field.
  • Step 9. Enter BMOBILE INTERNET in the Name field and tap on OK.
  • Step 10. Tap on the APN field.
  • Step 11. Enter internet in the APN field and tap on the OK option.
  • Step 12. Enter https://www.bmobile.com.pg and tap on the OK option.
  • Step 13. Tap on the APN type field.
  • Step 14. Tap on Internet.
  • Step 15. Tap on the Options icon on the handset.
  • Step 16. Tap on the Save option.
  • Step 17. Tap on the BMOBILE INTERNET selection circle.
  • Step 18. Your profile will be created and set as default.
