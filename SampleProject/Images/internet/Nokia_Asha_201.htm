Nokia Asha 201

  • Step 1. Press the Menu button.
  • Step 2. Highlight Settings and press the Select button.
  • Step 3. Highlight Configuration and press the Select button.
  • Step 4. Highlight Personal settings and press the Select button.
  • Step 5. Press the Add button.
  • Step 6. Highlight Web and press the Select button.
  • Step 7. Highlight Account name and press the Select button.
  • Step 8. Under the Account name field enter Bmobile Internet and press the OK button.
  • Step 9. Highlight Use preferred access pt and press the select button.
  • Step 10. Highlight No and press the Select button.
  • Step 11. Highlight Access point settings and press the Select button.
  • Step 12. Highlight Bearer settings and press the Select button.
  • Step 13. Highlight Packet data access pt and press the Select button.
  • Step 14. In the Access point field, enter internet and press the OK button.
  • Step 15. Press the Back button until you reach the Personal accounts screen.
  • Step 16. Highlight Bmobile Internet and press the Options button.
  • Step 17. Highlight Activate and press the Select button.
  • Step 18. Your manual internet will be configured.
