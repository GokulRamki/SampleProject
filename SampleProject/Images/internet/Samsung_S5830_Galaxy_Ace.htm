Samsung S5830 Galaxy Ace

  • Step 1. Press the Menu button.
  • Step 2. Tap on Settings.
  • Step 3. Tap on Wireless & network.
  • Step 4. Tap on Mobile networks.
  • Step 5. Tap on Access Point Names.
  • Step 6. Press the Menu button and tap on New APN
  • Step 7. Tap on Name and enter Bmobile Internet then tap OK.
  • Step 8. Tap on APN and enter internet and tap OK
  • Step 9. Tap on APN type and tap Internet.
  • Step 10. Press the Menu button and save.
  • Step 11. Tap on Bmobile Internet to select it.
  • Step 12. The internet has now been manually configured.
