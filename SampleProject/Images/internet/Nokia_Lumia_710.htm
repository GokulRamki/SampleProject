Nokia Lumia 710

  • Step 1 Start screen.
  • Step 2 Choose the arrow symbol
  • Step 3 Choose Settings.
  • Step 4 Scroll down.
  • Step 5 Choose mobile network.
  • Step 6 Scroll down.
  • Step 7 Choose add apn.
  • Step 8 Under APN, enter internet
  • Step 9 Under Username, NONE.
  • Step 10 Under Password, NONE
  • Step 11 Under Proxy server/URL, enter nothing.
  • Step 12 Under Proxy port, enter nothing.
  • Step 13 Choose the tick symbol.
  • Step 14 Return to the Start screen.
  • Step 15 Start screen.
  • Step 16 Switch the Nokia Lumia 710 off and then on again to activate the settings.
