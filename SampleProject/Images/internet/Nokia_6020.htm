Nokia 6020

  • Step 1. Press the Menu button.
  • Step 2. Highlight the Settings icon and press the Select button.
  • Step 3. Highlight Configuration settings and press the Select button.
  • Step 4. Highlight Personal configuration settings and press the Select button.
  • Step 5. Press the Add new button.
  • Step 6. Highlight Web and press the Select button.
  • Step 7. Highlight Account name and press the Select button.
  • Step 8. Enter Bmobile Internet and press the OK button.
  • Step 9. Highlight Homepage and press the Select button.
  • Step 10. Enter http://www.bmobile.com.pg and press the OK button.
    • Scroll down to Use preferred access point.
  • Step 11. Highlight Use preferred access point and press the Select button.
  • Step 12. Highlight No and press the Select button.
  • Step 13. Press the OK button.
  • Step 14. Highlight Access point settings and press the Select button.
  • Step 15. Highlight Bearer settings and press the Select button.
  • Step 16. Ensure Packet data access point is set to internet.
    • Press the Back button until you reach the Personal acct screen.
  • Step 17. Your Internet profile will now be configured.
