Samsung C3303

  • Step 1. Tap on Menu.
  • Step 2. Tap on the Internet icon.
  • Step 3. Tap on “NO” when asked if you want to connect to the internet
  • Step 4. Tap on the Options icon.
  • Step 5. Tap on Settings.
  • Step 6. Tap on Internet profiles.
  • Step 7. Tap on Create.
  • Step 8. Tap on Set name.
  • Step 9. Enter in Bmobile Internet and tap on Done.
  • Step 10. Tap on Home URL.
  • Step 11. Enter in http://www.bmobile.com.pg and tap on Done.
  • Step 13. Tap on GPRS settings.
  • Step 14. Tap on Access name.
  • Step 15. Enter in internet and tap on Done.
  • Step 16. Tap on Save.
  • Step 17. Tap on Bmobile Internet
  • Step 18. Your profile has been configured.
