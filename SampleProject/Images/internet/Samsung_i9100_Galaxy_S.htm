  • Step 1. Press the Menu button.
  • Step 2. Tap on Settings.
  • Step 3. Tap on Wireless and network.
  • Step 4. Tap on Mobile networks.
  • Step 5. Tap on Access Point Names.
  • Step 6. Press the Menu button.
  • Step 7. Tap on New APN.
  • Step 8. Tap on Name.
  • Step 9. Enter Bmobile Internet and tap on OK.
  • Step 10. Tap on APN.
  • Step 11. Enter in internet and tap on OK.
  • Step 12. Press the Menu button.
  • Step 13. Tap on Save.
  • Step 14. Tap on Bmobile Internet
  • Step 15. The internet has now been manually configured.
