Nokia Pureview 808

  • Step 1. Tap on Menu.
  • Step 2. Tap on Settings.
  • Step 3. Tap on Connectivity.
  • Step 4. Tap on Settings.
  • Step 5. Tap on Network destinations.
  • Step 6. Tap on Internet.
  • Step 7. Tap on Yes when asked to automatically search for available access points
  • Step 8. Tap on Mobile data.
  • Step 9. In the Mobile data access pt name: field, enter Bmobile Internet and tap on OK.
  • Step 10. Tap on Internet.
  • Step 11. Tap on Internet again.
  • Step 12. Tap on Bmobile Internet
  • Step 13. Tap on Access point name.
  • Step 14. In the Access point name text field, enter internet and tap on OK.
  • Step 15. Tap on Homepage.
  • Step 16. In the Homepage text field, enter https://www.bmobile.com.pg and tap on OK.
  • Step 17. Tap on the return icon until you return to the Internet screen.
  • Step 18. Tap and hold on Bmobile Wap
  • Step 19. Tap on Change priority.
  • Step 20. Tap on Priority: 1. Bmobile Internet will now be set as default connection.
