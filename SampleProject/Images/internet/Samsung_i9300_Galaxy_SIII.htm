Samsung i9300 Galaxy SIII (Android)

  • Step 1. Tap on the Menu icon.
  • Step 2. Tap on the Settings icon.
  • Step 3. Tap on More settings.
  • Step 4. Tap on the Mobile networks option.
  • Step 5. Tap on Access Point Names.
  • Step 6. Tap on the Menu icon.
  • Step 7. Tap on the New APN option.
  • Step 8. Tap on the Name field.
  • Step 9. Enter Bmobile Internet in the Name field and tap on the OK option.
  • Step 10. Tap on the APN field.
  • Step 11. Enter internet in the APN field and tap on the OK option.
  • Step 12. Tap on the Menu icon on the handset.
  • Step 13. Tap on the Save option.
  • Step 14. Tap on Bmobile Internet selection circle.
  • Step 15. Your profile will be created and set as default.
