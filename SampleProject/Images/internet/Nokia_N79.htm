Nokia N79

  • Step 2. Select the Tools icon.
  • Step 3. Select the Settings icon.

  • Step 5. Highlight Destinations and press the Open button.
  • Step 6. Highlight Internet and press the Open button.
  • Step 7. Press the Options button.
  • Step 8. Highlight New access point and press the Select button.
  • Step 9. Press the Yes button.
  • Step 10. The handset is searching for available access points.
  • Step 11. Highlight Packet data and press the Select button.
  • Step 12. Enter internet and press the OK button.
  • Step 13. The access point has been added.
  • Step 14. Press the Back button.
  • Step 15. Press the Options button.
  • Step 16. Highlight Default connection and press the Select button.
  • Step 17. Highlight Internet and press the Select button.
  • Step 18. Your access point has been set to default.
  • Step 19. Highlight Internet and press the Open button.
  • Step 20. Press the Options button.
  • Step 21. Highlight Edit and press the Select button.
  • Step 22. Under connection name enter Bmobile Internet and press the OK button.
  • Step 23. Ensure that the Access point name is internet..
  • Step 24. Highlight Homepage and press the Change button.
  • Step 25. Enter http://www.bmobile.com.pg and press the OK button. Press the OK button.
