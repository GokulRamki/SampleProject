HTC A9191 Desire HD

Start screen > Choose the arrow symbol > Choose Settings > Choose Wireless & networks > Choose Mobile network, and check this option. Choose Mobile networks > Choose Access Point Names > Choose the menu key, and choose New APN.

Under Name: enter Bmobile Internet.
Under APN: enter internet
Under Proxy: enter nothing.
Under Port: enter nothing.
Under User name: enter nothing.
Under Password: enter nothing.
Scroll down.
Under MCC: enter 537.
Under MNC: enter 01.
Under Authentication type, choose PAP.
Under APN type: enter default. (Note: in lower case)
Choose the menu key.
Choose Save.
Choose Bmobile Internet, and check this option.
Return to the Main menu.

Choose Internet > Choose the menu key > choose More > and then Settings > Choose Set homepage and Choose Set home page Enter http://www.bmobile.com.pg
Return to the Start screen.
Start screen.
Switch off the HTC Desire HD and restart it in order to activate the settings.
You can now start using the internet.

HTC Explorer A310B

Checking the data connection you're using
1. From the Home screen, press , and then tap Settings > Wireless & networks.
2. Tap Mobile networks > Network operators or Access Point Names.

Adding a new access point
Before you add another data connection, have the access point name and settings (including username and password if required) from your mobile operator ready.
1. From the Home screen, press , and then tap Settings > Wireless & networks.
2. Tap Mobile networks > Access Point Names.
3. On the APNs screen, press and then tap New APN.

Put in the following
• Name: Bmobile Internet
• APN: internet
• Proxy: Enter Nothing
• Port: Enter Nothing
• Username: Enter Nothing
• Password: Enter Nothing
• Server: Enter Nothing
• MMSC: Enter Nothing
• MMS Proxy: Enter Nothing
• MMS Port: Enter Nothing
• MCC: 537
• MNC: 01
• APN Type: default “if it has a selection choice internet”

4. Press and then tap Save.
