Samsung GT B3210 Corby TXT

  • Step 1. Press the Menu button.
  • Step 2. Highlight the Settings icon and press the Select button.
  • Step 3. Highlight Network Settings and press the Select button.
  • Step 4. Highlight Connection settings and press the Select button.
  • Step 5. Press the Options button.
  • Step 6. Highlight Create and press the Select button.
  • Step 7. In the Profile name field enter Bmobile Internet and in the Home URL field enter http://www.bmobile.com.pg
  • Step 8. Ensure that Bearer is set to GPRS, then highlight the Advanced settings and press the Center button.
  • Step 9. In the APN field enter internet then Save the changes.
  • Step 10. Your manual internet profile will now be configured.
