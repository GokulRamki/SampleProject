Samsung E250

  • Step 1. Press the Menu button.
  • Step 2. Highlight the Browser icon and press the Select button.
  • Step 3. Highlight Profile settings and press the Select button.
  • Step 4. Highlight an available profile and press the Options button.
  • Step 5. Highlight Edit and press the Select button.
  • Step 6. Under the Profile name field, enter BMOBILE INTERNET
  • Step 7. Under the Home URL field, enter http://www.bmobile.com.pg
  • Step 8. Ensure that the Bearer is set to GPRS only.
  • Step 9. Ensure that the Proxy is set to Disable.
  • Step 10. Under the APN field enter internet and press the Options button.
  • Step11. Highlight Save and press the Select button.
  • Step12. Press the Save button again.
  • Step13. Highlight BMOBILE INTERNET and press the Centre button.
  • Step14. The profile will be changed.
  • Step15. Your Internet profile has been configured.
