Nokia C1 - 02

  • Step 1. Press the Menu button.
  • Step 2. Highlight Settings and press the Select button.
  • Step 3. Highlight Configuration and press the Select button.
  • Step 4. Highlight Personal configuration settings and press the Select button.
  • Step 5. Press the Options button.
  • Step 6. Highlight Add new and press the Select button.
  • Step 7. Highlight Web and press the Select button.
  • Step 8. Highlight Account name and press the Select button.
  • Step 9. Under the Account name field enter Bmobile Internet and press the OK button.
  • Step 10. Highlight Homepage and press the Select button.
  • Step 11. Under the Homepage field enter http://www.bmobile.com.pg and press the OK button.
  • Step 12. Highlight Use preferred access point and move right to change status to No.
  • Step 13. Highlight Access point settings and press the Select button.
  • Step 14. Highlight Bearer settings and press the Select button.
  • Step 15. Ensure that the Packet data access point is set to internet and press the Back button until you reach the Personal accts screen.
  • Step 16. Highlight Bmobile Internet and press the Options button.
  • Step 17. Highlight Activate and press the Select button.
  • Step 18. Your new profile has been created and set as default.
