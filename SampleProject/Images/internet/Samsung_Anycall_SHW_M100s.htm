Samsung Anycall SHW M100s

  • Step 1. From the main menu, select "Settings" and then select "Network"
  • Step 2. Select Connections and then "Yes" when prompted to continue.
  • Step 3. Tap on the Options menu and select "Create"
  • Step 4. Tap on Set Name and type "Bmobile Internet" and then tap "Done"
  • Step 5. Select Home URL and enter http://www.bmobile.com.pg and then tap "Done"
  • Step 6. Select Access Name and enter "internet" and then tap "Done"
  • Step 7. Save and press the Back button until you reach the main menu screen.
  • Step 8. Tap on the internet icon
  • Step 9. Tap on the Options icon located at the bottom right hand side of the screen.
  • Step 10. Tap on Settings.
  • Step 11. Tap on Internet profiles.
  • Step 12. Tap on Bmobile Internet
  • Step 13.Tap on Save.
  • Your profile will be selected.
