  • Step 1. Tap on the Applications icon.
  • Step 2. Tap on the Settings icon.
  • Step 3. Tap on Wireless and network.

  • Step 5. Tap on Access Point Names.
  • Step 6. Tap on Menu.
  • Step 7. Tap on New APN.

  • Step 9. Enter Bmobile Internet in the Name field and then tap on OK.

  • Step 11. Enter internet in the APN field and then tap on OK.
  • Step 12. Tap on the Authentication Type
  • Step 13. Tap on PAP.
  • Step 14. Tap on the APN type field.
  • Step 15. Tap on Internet.
  • Step 16. Tap on Menu.
  • Step 17. Tap on Save.
  • Step 18. Your profile will be created and set as default.
