Nokia 5730

  • Step 1. Press the Menu button.
  • Step 2. Highlight the Settings icon and press the Open button.
  • Step 3. Highlight Settings and press the Open button.
  • Step 4. Highlight Connection and press the Open button.
  • Step 5. Highlight Destinations and press the Open button.
  • Step 6. Highlight Internet and press the Open button.
  • Step 7. Press the Options button.
  • Step 8. Highlight new access point and press the Select button.
  • Step 9. Press the No button.
  • Step 10. Highlight Packet data and press the Select button.
  • Step 11. Type in BMOBILE INTERNET and press the OK button.
  • Step 12. New access point for Internet has been added.
  • Step 13. Highlight BMOBILE INTERNET and press the Edit button.
  • Step 14. Highlight Access point name and press the Change button.
  • Step 15. Enter internet and press the OK button.
  • Step 16. Highlight Homepage and press the Change button.
  • Step 17. Enter http://www.bmobile.com.pgand press the OK button.
  • Step 18. Press the Back button until you reach the internet screen.
  • Step 19. Highlight BMOBILE INTERNET and press the Options button.
  • Step 20. Highlight Organise and press the Select button.
  • Step 21. Highlight Change priority and press the Select button.
  • Step 22. Highlight Internet. GPRS and press the Centre button.
  • Step 23. BMOBILE INTERNET will now be changed to Priority: 1. Your manual internet profile will now be configured.
