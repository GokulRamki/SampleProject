Samsung i9100 Galaxy SII

  • Step 1. Tap on the Applications icon.
  • Step 2. Tap on the Settings icon.
  • Step 3. Tap on Wireless & network.
  • Step 4. Tap on Mobile networks.
  • Step 5. Tap on Access Point Names.
  • Step 6. Tap on the Options icon on the handset.
  • Step 7. Tap on New APN.
  • Step 8. Tap on Name.
  • Step 9. In the Name text field enter Bmobile Internet and tap on the OK option.
  • Step 10. Tap on APN.
  • Step 11. In the APN text field enter internet and tap on OK.
  • Step 12. Tap on APN type.
  • Step 13. Tap on Internet.
  • Step 14. Tap on the Options icon on the handset.
  • Step 15. Tap on Save.
  • Step 16. Tap on the Bmobile Internet selection circle.
  • Step 17. Your manual internet has been configured and set as default.
