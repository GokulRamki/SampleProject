Nokia N70

  • Step 1. Press the Menu button.
  • Step 2. Highlight the Tools icon and press the Centre button.
  • Step 3. Highlight the Settings icon and press the Centre button.
  • Step 4. Highlight Connection and press the Centre button.
  • Step 5. Highlight the Access point and press the Centre button.
  • Step 6. Press the Options button.
  • Step 7. Highlight New access point and press the Centre button.
  • Step 8. Highlight Use default settings and press the Centre button.
  • Step 9. Highlight Connection name and press the Centre button.
  • Step 10. In the Text field, enter Bmobile Internet and press the OK button.
  • Step 11. Highlight Access point name and press the Centre button.
  • Step 12. In the Text field, enter internet and press the OK button.
  • Step 13. Scroll down to Homepage and press the Centre button.
  • Step 14. In the Text field, enter http://www.bmobile.com.pg and press the OK button.
  • Step 15. Save and return to main menu home screen.
  • Step 16. Press the Menu button.
  • Step 17. Highlight Web and press the Centre button.
  • Step 18. Press the Options button.
  • Step 19. Highlight Settings and press the Centre button.
  • Step 20. Highlight Access point and press the Centre button.
  • Step 21. Highlight Bmobile Internet and press the Centre button. The Default access point is set.
