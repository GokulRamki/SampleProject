Nokia E71

  • Step 1. Press the Menu button.
  • Step 2. Highlight the Tools icon and press the Centre button.
  • Step 3 Highlight the Settings icon and press the Centre button.
  • Step 4. Highlight Connection and press the Centre button
  • Step 5. Highlight Access points and press the Centre button.
  • Step 6. Highlight New access point and press the Select button.
  • Step 7. Enter the Connection Name, Bmobile Internet in the text field.
    • Enter the Access point, internet in the text field.
  • Step 8. Scroll down and enter the homepage, http://www.bmobile.com.pg in the text field.
    • Press the Options button.
  • Step 9. Go back to the main menu
  • Step 10. Highlight the Web icon and press the Centre button.
  • Step 11. Highlight Settings and press the Select button.
  • Step 12. Highlight General and press the Centre button.
  • Step 13. Highlight Access point and press the Centre button.
  • Step 14. Scroll down to User defined and press the Centre button.
  • Step 15. Highlight Bmobile Internet and press the OK to set Profile to Default.
  • Step 16. Manual configuration is complete.
