Samsung C5212

  • Step 1. From the menu button, highlight the Browser icon and press the Select button.
  • Step 2. Highlight settings and then highlight Browser profiles and press the Select button.
  • Step 3. Highlight an available profile and press the Options button.
  • Step 4. Highlight Edit and press the Select button.
  • Step 5. Enter the following information:
    • Name: Bmobile internet
    • Home URL: http://www.bmobile.com.pg
  • Step 6. Ensure that the Bearer is set to GPRS only.
  • Step 7. Highlight GPRS settings and press the center button.
  • Step 8. Under the Access name field enter internet and press the Options button
  • Step 9. Press the Save button again to save changes to the profile.
  • Step 10. Highlight Bmobile Internet; press the center button and then the Options button.
  • Step 11. Highlight Save and press the Select button. The Current profile will be changed.
    • Your Internet profile has now been configured and set to default.
