Nokia E63

  • Step 1. Press the Menu button.
  • Step 2. Highlight the Tools icon and press the center button.
  • Step 3. Highlight the Settings icon and press the center button.
  • Step 4. Highlight Connection and press the center button.
  • Step 5. Highlight Access Points and press the center button.
  • Step 6. Press the Options button.
  • Step 7. Highlight New Access Point and press the Select button.
  • Step 8. Highlight Connection Name and press the center button.
  • Step 9. Type in Bmobile Internet in the text field and press the OK button.
  • Step 10. Highlight Access Point Name and press the center button.
  • Step 11. Type in internet in the text field and press the OK button.
  • Step 12. Highlight Homepage and press the center button.
  • Step 13. Type in http://www.bmobile.com.pg in the text field and press the OK button.
  • Step 14. The Internet profile has been created.
    • Press the back button till you reach the Main Menu screen.
  • Step 15. Press the Menu button.
  • Step 16. Highlight the Web icon and press the center button.
  • Step 17. Press the Options button.
  • Step 18. Highlight Settings and press the Select button.
  • Step 19. Highlight General and press the center button.
  • Step 20. Highlight Access Point and press the center button.
  • Step 21. Highlight User defined and press the OK button.
  • Step 22. Highlight Bmobile Internet and press the OK button.
  • Step 23. Manual Internet has been configured.
