Samsung GT S8600 Wave 3

  • Step 1. Tap on Menu.
  • Step 2. Tap on Settings.
  • Step 3. Tap on Network.
  • Step 4. Tap on Connections.
  • Step 5. Tap on Yes when prompted to continue
  • Step 6. Tap on New.
  • Step 7. Enter Bmobile Internet and then tap on access name.
  • Step 8. Enter internet and then scroll down to Home URL.
  • Step 9. Tap on Home URL and enter www.bmobile.com.pg and then save.
  • Step 10. Your manual internet will be configured.
