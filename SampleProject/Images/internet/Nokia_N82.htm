Nokia N82

  • Step 1. Press the Menu button.
  • Step 2. Highlight Tools and press the Center button.
  • Step 3. Highlight Settings and press the Center button.
  • Step 4. Highlight Connection and press the Center button.
  • Step 5. Highlight Access points and press the Center button.
  • Step 6. Press the Options button.
  • Step 7. Highlight New access point and press the Select button.
  • Step 8. Highlight Connection name and press the Center button.
  • Step 9. Enter in Bmobile Internet and press the OK button.
  • Step 10. Ensure Data bearer is set as Packet data.
    • Scroll down to Access point name and press the Center button.
  • Step 11. Enter in internet and press the OK button.
  • Step 12. Ensure the Username and Password fields blank.
    • Ensure Authentication is set as secure.
  • Step 13. Scroll down to Homepage and press the Center button.
  • Step 14. Enter http://www.bmobile.com.pg and press the OK button.
  • Step 15. Press the Back button until you reach the Access points screen.
  • Step 16. Your profile will now be created.
